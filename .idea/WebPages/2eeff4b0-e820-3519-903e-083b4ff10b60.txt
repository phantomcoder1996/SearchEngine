        <span itemprop="description"></span> 
        <div itemprop="articleBody"> 
         <p>CSS is used to control the style of a web document in a simple and easy way.</p>
         <p>CSS is the acronym for "Cascading Style Sheet". This online training covers both the versions CSS1,CSS2 and CSS3, and gives a complete understanding of CSS, starting from its basics to advanced concept</p>
