        <p>XML stands for <b>Ex</b>tensible <b>M</b>arkup <b>L</b>anguage and is a text-based markup language derived from Standard Generalized Markup Language (SGML). This tutorial will teach you the basics of XML. The tutorial is divided into sections such as XML Basics, Advanced XML, and XML tools. Each of these sections contain related topics with simple and useful examples.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>This reference has been prepared for beginners to help them understand the basic to advanced concepts related to XML. This tutorial will give you enough understanding on XML from where you can take yourself to a higher level of expertise.</p>
