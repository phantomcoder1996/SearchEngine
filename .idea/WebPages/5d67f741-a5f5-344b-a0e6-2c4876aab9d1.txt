        <p>HyperSQL Database is a modern relational database manager that conforms closely to the SQL:2011 standard and JDBC 4 specifications. It supports all core features and RDBMS. HSQLDB is used for the development, testing, and deployment of database applications. In this tutorial, we will look closely at HSQLDB, which is one of the best open-source, multi-model, next generation NoSQL product.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>This tutorial is designed for Software Professionals who are willing to learn HSQL Database in simple and easy steps. It will give you a great understanding on HSQLDB concepts.</p> 
        <h1>Prerequisites</h1> 
        <p>Before you start practicing the various types of examples given in this tutorial, we assume you are already aware of the concepts of database, especially RDBMS.</p>
